Internal display flickers intermittently or goes blank after reassembly; HDMI output unaffected. Likely cause: Damaged or mis-seated J24 MIPI-DSI flex cable (30-pin, 0.5 mm pitch) between CPU module and SN65DSI86 eDP bridge, or Wi-Fi antenna routing interference into display path. Under microscope inspect J24 ZIF pads for lifted contacts; re-seat or re-flow. reflow U10 if suspected cold joint.
